⚙ Optimization Parameters

How the Algorithm Works

Step 1 — EDF HeuristicSort tasks by Score = w₁×(1/deadline) + w₂×priority → assign to earliest available slot.
Step 2 — ILP Re-optimizationMinimize Z = ∑Lᵢ subject to assignment, capacity, lateness bounds, precedence, and daily caps.
Step 3 — Stochastic BufferInflate durations: p'ᵢ = E[pᵢ] + α·σ(pᵢ). α=1.5 gives 90th-percentile coverage.
Step 4 — Dynamic ReschedulingIf overrun >10 min, re-optimize the disruption window and notify all affected scientists.
